Privacy Policyv1.0

This Privacy Policy explains how PurpleMusic ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate the app:

  • Account Data: Pi Network username, wallet address, internal user ID.
  • Premium Status: Whether you have an active premium plan and its expiration timestamp.
  • Usage Signals: Non-identifying technical events (e.g. playlist interactions) for reliability & UX improvements.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide core functionality (playlists, likes, premium gating).
  • To process premium access after Pi payment verification.
  • To maintain app security and prevent abuse.

We do not sell your data.

3. Premium & Payments

When you initiate a Pi payment we receive only the metadata required to confirm completion, plan, and amount. We do not store your private keys.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We may use localStorage for session or UI preferences (e.g. cached user row, premium state). No third‑party ad trackers are used.

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while you actively use the service. Premium flags auto‑expire after the plan duration elapses.

6. Security

We rely on Supabase managed infrastructure and apply least privilege to service keys. Still, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us for removal.

8. International Transfers

Data may be processed in regions where Supabase infrastructure operates. By using the App you consent to such processing.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy. Updated versions are indicated by the version badge above. Material changes may be announced in‑app.

10. Contact

Questions or requests: support@purplemusic.app
